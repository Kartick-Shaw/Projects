Khana Khazana

About Us

Introducing one of the finest restaurants in town, renowned for its exceptional food, warm hospitality, and exquisite ambiance - Khana Khazana. Established in 2005, Khana Khazana has become a go-to destination for food lovers, offering a unique culinary experience that tantalizes the taste buds. The restaurant is situated in a prime location, with a stunning view of the city's skyline, making it an ideal spot for romantic dinners, family gatherings, and corporate events. The restaurant's menu is a fusion of traditional and contemporary cuisine, with each dish prepared using only the freshest and highest quality ingredients. Whether you're a vegetarian or a meat lover, you're sure to find something to your liking, with an extensive range of appetizers, entrees, and desserts to choose from. The kitchen is headed by an award-winning chef, who brings years of experience and creativity to every dish. Khana Khazana is not just about the food, though. The restaurant's interior is tastefully decorated, with an elegant and modern design that creates a warm and inviting atmosphere. The lighting is subdued, creating a relaxed and intimate ambiance, perfect for a romantic dinner. The restaurant also boasts an impressive wine list, carefully curated to complement the menu's diverse flavors. The staff at Khana Khazana are friendly and professional, always ready to go above and beyond to make your dining experience memorable. Whether you're a regular or a first-time guest, you'll receive the same impeccable service that the restaurant is renowned for. Overall, Khana Khazana is a place where food, service, and ambiance come together to create an unforgettable dining experience. From the moment you walk in, you'll be greeted with a warm welcome, and from the first bite to the last, you'll savor every moment.
